Volleyball Galatica

Your divice doesn't have enough width or is not supported for this game preview

Description -

The core premise is simple; similar to volleyball you must get the ball to the ground on your opponent's side whilst keeping it off your ground. Can you stand up to the challenge? And will you be taking it alone? Press start, add as many players as you would like (max 4), click their name to set it to AI if necessary, and find out.

Controls -

Player 1:
A and D to move left and right
W to jump
E to use your ultimate ability

Player 2 (Optional):
LEFT and RIGHT ARROWS to move left and right
UP ARROW to jump
RIGHT SHIFT to use your ultimate ability

Objective -

As a player, you have 2 primary objectives:

  • First and formost the goal is to keep the ball away from the ground on your side. If it does, you will concede a point and being as you only have 5 as indicated on the top each one is critical.
  • A secondary objective is one that contributes to the primary -- you can push in the buttons on the bottom in order to gain your ultimate! Using this, depending on the selection you set in the lobby you will have 2 options to choose from depicted in the 3rd page of the "How to play" secton
